Koramangala is one of Bangalore's most sought-after neighborhoods, known for its cosmopolitan vibe and bustling energy. It's a major startup hub, attracting a young, professional crowd.

Lifestyle and Vibe:
The area is famous for its wide array of restaurants, pubs, cafes, and breweries, making it a hotspot for foodies and nightlife enthusiasts. You'll find everything from high-end dining to quirky food trucks. The streets are often busy, especially in the evenings and on weekends. It's a very social and active neighborhood.

Rent and Housing:
Rental prices in Koramangala are on the higher side due to its popularity and central location. A typical 1BHK can range from ₹20,000 to ₹30,000, while a 2BHK often goes for ₹35,000 to ₹50,000+. You'll find a mix of independent houses, modern apartment complexes, and builder floors.

Pros:
Excellent connectivity to other major parts of the city like HSR Layout, Indiranagar, and the Central Business District (CBD).
Unmatched options for dining and entertainment.
Vibrant, youthful, and energetic atmosphere.

Cons:
Can be crowded and noisy.
Traffic congestion is a significant issue, especially during peak hours.
Higher cost of living compared to other areas.
